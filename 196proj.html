WASTEBUSTERS

Wasted Food, SOLVED!

College students everywhere waste literal tons of food each year because food is often left to rot in the fridge.

How did we do it?

We propose to install a camera in the fridge, capturing images of perishables and keeping track of when they perish via iOS application.
